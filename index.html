About Us_

Bees Logistics is not just another logistics solution; it is a game-changer. Our mission is to elevate the logistics landscape in Preston, empowering businesses and individuals alike to thrive in this dynamic and growing sector. For last-mile companies, Bees Logistics provides you with an operating system to manage your entire business from one central place. You can manage everything from your riders to your orders, deliveries, and payments. Droply has got you covered! Beyond managing your business, Bees Logistics provides you with access to financing to expand your business, access electric vehicles, insure your goods and provide your riders with insurance.
